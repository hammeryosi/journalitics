Welcome to Journalitics!

This is a project I prepared during my fellowship in The Data Incubator. The idea is to use the Stanford natural language processing tool coreNLP to study the content of major newspapers.

In the current version, I analyzed headlines from the 4 largest newspapers in New York, along with article summaries. I extracted the names of people appearing in the text using coreNLP's Named Entity Recognition annotator. You can see the top names mentioned in article headlines for a chosen time range, and the relative frequencies in which they appear in each paper.

Enjoy!
